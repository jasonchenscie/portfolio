Gratitude Pin

A conceptual design for gratitude practice

OVERVIEW

As a designer and video producer, I led a team of 2 and developed a concept for Gratitude pin, a pin that can be carried around or worn by the users. It is a simple and easy-to-use pin that has a big button on it. By pushing the button, users could record whatever they say. However, this is not to let users record random words, but appreciations they have to the objects around them. The pin data is connected with users’ online account that allows them to track their appreciations.

Gratitude is good for you

Drawing from large amount of research and ancient philosophy literature, we decided to design a system that could help people practice gratitude.

The Problem

As indicated by literature, recording one's appreciation to surrounding is key in gratitude practice. However, recording one's appreciation can be inconvenient because one needs to carry pen and paper with them.

The Concept

Instead of using pen and paper, we imagined an internet-connected, voice-recording pin that could be worn on one's clothes. The pin was made meaningful by allowing users to customize the pin design with a personally significant picture. Together with a mobile application that synced with the pin, the users could record their appreciations everywhere. The pin served as a physical reminder as well as a special recorder for appreciations.
